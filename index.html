Crystal Game

Here is how you play the game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get" There are five crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
